# Gareth Chong's Desk

The website encapsulates who I am as a person, my qualifications and my personal life both.

**Live site:** [personal-website-virid-nine-43.vercel.app]

---

## Tech Stack

- **Framework:** React
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS
- **Deployment:** Vercel
- **Fonts:** [Fontshare](https://fontshare.com) — Erode (display), Switzer (body)

---

## Content Pattern: Desk Objects

Each object on the desk (resume, namecard, laptop, cards, photo, post-its, switch, patch, tennis ball) is its own component, imported independently and rendered in `Desk.jsx`. Each holds a single, fixed block of content rather than a list — so any object can be added, removed, or swapped out on its own without affecting the others.

---

## Design Notes

The desk surface went through several rounds of exploration — different wood textures, patterns, and shades of brown — before settling on a flat white. It gave the cleanest, most modern flat-lay look and made every object (regardless of color) read clearly against the background.
